Scrambled Eggs

Description

Scrambled eggs! A classic dish from practically everywhere across the world, it's just something to get you by. Its eggs seasoned with salt and pepper while scrambled in butter or any type of oil. Everybody knows how to make this but lets dive into how I make mines!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
      I.e Goose Eggs Chicken Eggs etc...
  • Green Onion
  • Pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Create the mixture: crack any quantity of eggs and dump its remains into a bowl.
    2. Season the mixture: use scissors to cut green onions into the bowl, then add salt and pepper.
  2. Mix Now take chopsticks or a stirrer and start whipping the eggs in a circular motion.
  3. Scramble the Eggs: Finally add oil to a pan, and turn the heat to it's highest level. Now wait until the oil is "ready". You can tell by poking chopsticks onto a pan, and if it sputters you know it's ready!
    4. Create Servings: Now pour the eggs into the pan, wait for it to form a slightly solid shape then start folding and breaking the eggs apart.
  4. Enjoy: After 2 minutes of scrambling add a final coat of salt and voila you have a plate of scrambled eggs.
